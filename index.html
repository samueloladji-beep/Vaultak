A kill switch
for rogue
AI agents.

Your AI agents have access to your systems, databases, and APIs. What happens when one goes rogue? AgentBreaker monitors every action in real time and stops it before the damage is done.

Watch every action

Sleep at night
If an agent goes rogue, AgentBreaker stops it automatically. Alert, pause, or roll back — you choose.
